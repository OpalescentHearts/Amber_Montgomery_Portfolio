Unit 6 - Memory Game

For the sixth unit we learned Javascript. As part of the unit project, My group decided to create a memory card game. Players can click or tap on cards to flip them over, and if they match a pair, the cards will stay flipped over. If they do not match, the cards will flip back over, and the player must try again all while a timer ticks down.
